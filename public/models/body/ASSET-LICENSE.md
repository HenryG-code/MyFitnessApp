# Anatomical human model

`logfit-anatomical-human.glb` is derived from the **Body Male Realistic**
model in the Blender Foundation's Human Base Meshes Bundle.

- Source: https://download.blender.org/demo/bundles/bundles-3.6/
- License: [Creative Commons CC0 1.0 Universal](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/)
- Copyright status: dedicated to the public domain; attribution is not required

## LogFit modifications

The source model was evaluated at its first Multiresolution level, centered at
the world origin, given neutral application-specific eye and skin materials,
exported to glTF 2.0, and optimized with glTF Transform 4.4.1 using Meshopt
compression and mesh quantization. No texture files or third-party materials
are included.

This file is kept with the app to make the model's provenance and
redistribution terms explicit.
